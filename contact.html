Common Questions

Before you reach out, some answers

How does the process start?

After you fill in the contact form, Ru will reach out within 24 hours to arrange a free 30-minute discovery call — no commitment required. We'll discuss your goals, challenges, and whether we're a good fit.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes — we regularly work with clients in the UK, USA, and Australia. All communication and project management is handled remotely via video calls, collaborative tools, and detailed documentation.

How long does a typical project take?

It depends on scope. A UX audit typically takes 1–2 weeks. A full website design and build can range from 4–10 weeks. We'll agree a timeline before starting and keep you updated throughout.

What if I'm not sure what I need?

That's completely fine — and more common than you think. Just describe where you are and what problem you're trying to solve. We'll help you figure out the right approach during the discovery call.

Do you offer mentoring for beginners?

Absolutely. Mentoring sessions are available for designers at all levels — from students and career switchers through to senior designers wanting a fresh perspective. Sessions are tailored to your goals.

How do payments work?

Projects are typically split into a 50% deposit upfront and 50% on completion. For longer projects, staged payments can be arranged. All pricing is confirmed in writing before work begins.
